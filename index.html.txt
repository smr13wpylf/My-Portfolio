          <h2 class="section__title">How I <em>Think</em></h2>

          <p class="method__lead">
            The frameworks I use aren't belief systems I'm selling. They're the tools 
            that helped me survive — and then helped me make sense of what I survived.
          </p>

          <div class="method__frameworks">
            <div class="method__framework">
              <div class="method__framework-icon">♌︎</div>
              <div class="method__framework-text">
                <h4>Astrology</h4>
                <p>Not as fate, but as a map of psychological and archetypal territory. I use it to track 
                patterns, understand timing, and contextualize what's happening at the collective level.</p>
              </div>
            </div>
            <div class="method__framework">
              <div class="method__framework-icon">⬟</div>
              <div class="method__framework-text">
                <h4>Human Design</h4>
                <p>The mechanics of how we're wired to move, decide, and metabolize experience. 
                Particularly useful for understanding why traditional models of productivity, success, 
                and self-improvement don't work for everyone.</p>
              </div>
            </div>
            <div class="method__framework">
              <div class="method__framework-icon">🝓</div>
              <div class="method__framework-text">
                <h4>Gene Keys</h4>
                <p>The deeper archetypal layer beneath Human Design — mapping the shadow, the gift, 
                and the Siddhi of each gate. Where the psychological meets the mythic.</p>
              </div>
            </div>
            <div class="method__framework">
              <div class="method__framework-icon">7</div>
              <div class="method__framework-text">
                <h4>Numerology & Pattern Analysis</h4>
                <p>Numbers as energetic codes — not fortune-telling, but frequency mapping. 
                Particularly useful in understanding life cycles, karmic echoes, and timing.</p>
              </div>
            </div>
            <div class="method__framework">
              <div class="method__framework-icon">◈</div>
              <div class="method__framework-text">
                <h4>Shadow Work & Depth Psychology</h4>
                <p>The refusal to bypass. Looking at what's uncomfortable, repetitive, and 
                protected — because that's usually where the actual material is. 
                Influenced by Jung, but tested in real life first.</p>
              </div>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>

        <div class="method__right">
          <div class="method__principles">
            <h3 class="method__principles-title">Core Principles</h3>
            <ol class="method__principles-list">
              <li>
                <span class="method__num">01</span>
                <div>
                  <strong>Truth before comfort.</strong> 
                  Surface-level peace that requires you to ignore what you know is still a cage. I'd rather 
                  give you something that unsettles and clarifies than something that soothes and keeps you stuck.
                </div>
              </li>
              <li>
                <span class="method__num">02</span>
                <div>
                  <strong>Process over performance.</strong> 
                  I show the messy middle — the doubt, the wrestling, the pivots — because that's where 
                  actual growth lives. Not the clean before-and-after.
                </div>
              </li>
              <li>
                <span class="method__num">03</span>
                <div>
                  <strong>Wisdom is earned, not credentialed.</strong> 
                  I trust lived experience. I trust people who've been tested by their own ideas. 
                  I'm deeply skeptical of anyone who's never had to doubt what they believe.
                </div>
              </li>
              <li>
                <span class="method__num">04</span>
                <div>
                  <strong>Discernment over conversion.</strong> 
                  I'm not here to hand you a new belief system. I'm here to sharpen your ability 
                  to think for yourself. Take what resonates. Leave what doesn't. That's the deal.
                </div>
              </li>
              <li>
                <span class="method__num">05</span>
                <div>
                  <strong>Rebellion with a cause.</strong> 
                  Not nihilistic, not bitter. Questioning the systems without burning down everything 
                  that might still be good. Standing against what needs to be stood against, 
                  while staying soft enough to keep learning.
                </div>
              </li>
            </ol>
          </div>
